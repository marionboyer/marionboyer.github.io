Marion Boyer

I am a third year Ph.D. student at Inria in the Titane team, under the supervision of Florent Lafarge. My research is co-funded and supported by the French Space Agency (CNES) and Airbus Defense and Space.
My research combines the topics of computer vision and remote sensing. My current project focuses on building reconstruction from satellite images by improving each step of the pipeline.

I graduated with a Master's-equivalent diploma from the engineering school ENSEIRB-MATMECA in electronics with a specialization on image and signal processing in 2022. I did my final year internship at the French Space Agency (CNES) on matching of stereo images.
